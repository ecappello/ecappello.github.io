ABOUT

Eric Cappello is a young, award-winning composer and sound designer. In addition to a host of video games and films, his rapidly growing body of work includes concert music – orchestral and chamber works – multi-genre songs and ambient, electronic music.

Originally self-taught, his musical background now includes a Bachelor's from UCLA in composition and sound engineering, completed in June 2014.

Since graduation he has continued to work on various video games and films.
In addition to contract sound design work with NA3M Games ,  some notable recent credits include scoring the feature film Luggage (2014), and serving as Audio Lead on the upcoming VR game Classroom Aquatic .
Eric also works in-house as a sound designer for 7 Generation Games in Santa Monica.
